Fixed braces for comprehensive corrections.

Reliable and effective solutions for complex teeth corrections in Essen.

Traditional brackets for proven results.

Traditional brackets are a proven method for correcting dental misalignments, impressing with their reliability and effectiveness.

Robust and effective treatment for complex dental misalignments.

Precise control of tooth movement for optimal results in treatment for adolescents and adults.

Proven technology with a long history of success in orthodontics.

Self-ligating brackets for more comfort.

Self-ligating brackets offer a modern alternative to traditional brackets, as the treatment proceeds faster and more comfortably.

Less friction and a more pleasant wearing experience make this method particularly attractive.

Lower maintenance and easier handling contribute to a positive experience.

Better hygiene due to the absence of rubber bands is a significant advantage.

Lingual brackets for a completely discreet solution.

Lingual brackets are attached to the inner side of the teeth, making them nearly invisible.

An aesthetic solution without visible appliances for elegant teeth correction.

No impact on external appearance during treatment.

Ideal for patients who value discretion, especially for adult braces.

Removable braces for flexible use.

Flexible and comfortable options for discreet treatment are available with us.

Invisible aligners for modern teeth correction.

Clear aligners provide a transparent, removable solution for correcting dental misalignments and are especially popular among adults.

Nearly invisible for a discreet wearing experience with good results.

Removable for easy cleaning and eating – ideal for modern lifestyles.

Comfortable and tailored for each patient.

Removable braces for perfect treatment of children.

Removable braces are ideal for children and adolescents in their growth phase to correct mild to moderate misalignments.

Customizable and growth-supportive for the best possible results.

Easy handling and maintenance make these solutions particularly practical.

Ideal for the early stages of teeth correction – especially for children's braces.

Special braces for special requirements.

Individual solutions for specific orthodontic challenges.

Palatal expanders for dental space enlargement.

Palatal expanders are used to gently widen the upper jaw and create space for proper tooth alignment.

Effective in correcting crowding in the upper jaw – a crucial component of jaw health.

Supports harmonious facial development and correct tooth positioning.

Early correction of jaw misalignments through targeted measures.

Removable retainers for lasting success.

Retainers stabilize the teeth after the active treatment phase to secure the treatment results long-term.

Secures the position of the corrected teeth and prevents relapse.

Discreet and comfortable – the best choice for your dental health.

Important for the long-term maintenance of treatment success.

Fixed retainers for secure tooth positioning.

Fixed retainers are discreet wires attached to the back of the teeth to stabilize the corrected position.

Effective in securing tooth position – especially for adolescents.

Discreet and long-lasting, ideal for a permanent solution.

Important for the long-term maintenance of treatment success.
